Simple & Pure

How We Make It

No machines. No chemicals. Just hands, herbs, and time.

01

We Pick Fresh Herbs

We harvest our plants early in the morning when they are fresh. We use real leaves, roots, and flowers from local farmers, not dried powders.

02

We Cook Slowly

We mix herbs with oil and cook them slowly in large copper pots. We never use high heat because it destroys the vitamins. The copper cleans the oil naturally.

03

We Let It Rest

Once cooked, we let the oil sit in sunlight for 21 days. This slow process allows the oil to fully absorb the nutrients, making it powerful and ready for your skin.
